# 📊 JobSkills Trend Analyzer

A data analysis project that explores LinkedIn job post insights to uncover trending job roles, hiring patterns, experience levels, and more.

---

## 📌 Questions Answered

1. What skills are mentioned most in job posts?  
2. What are most hiring industries?  
3. What experience levels are in demand?  
4. What are most in demand job functions?  
5. Which locations have the highest job openings?

---

## 📁 Dataset

- 📄 File: `linkedin_job_posts_insights.xlsx`
- 📌 Contains 31,597 job listings with features like:
  - Job Title
  - Company Name
  - Location
  - Hiring Status
  - Date
  - Seniority Level
  - Job Function
  - Employment Type
  - Industry

## ✨ Result Summary

- 👨‍💻 Most in-demand job: **Software Developer**  
- 🏢 Leading industries: **Technology & IT**  
- 🎯 Most preferred experience: **Mid-Senior Level**  
- 🌍 Bengaluru & Karnataka are top hiring locations  
- 🏡 Remote jobs are strong, but on-site leads
